FAQ

Q: How do I know if I need a new roof?
A: Signs include leaks, missing shingles, or a roof older than 20 years.

Q: How often should I have my roof inspected?
A: Most experts recommend inspecting your roof at least once a year, ideally in the spring or fall. Regular inspections help identify small issues early, prevent costly damage, and extend the lifespan of your roof.
