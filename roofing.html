Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical roof last?

The average roof lasts 20-30 years depending on material, weather, and maintenance. Metal and tile roofs can last even longer.

What are the signs that my roof needs repair?

Common signs include missing shingles, interior leaks, sagging areas, and granules in gutters. Schedule a professional inspection to confirm.

Should I repair or replace my roof after a storm?

It depends on damage severity. Minor issues can often be repaired, but widespread damage usually requires full replacement.
