10 Signs You Need a New Roof

Don't wait until it's too late

Ignoring roof problems leads to costly damage. Here are the key signs that indicate it's time for a replacement:

  1. Curling or buckling shingles – Shingles that curl at edges signal advanced wear
  2. Missing shingles – Gaps expose your roof to water damage
  3. Granules in gutters – Excessive granules indicate deteriorating shingles
  4. Daylight through roof boards – Visible light means holes exist
  5. Sagging roof deck – A sagging roof indicates structural problems
  6. Persistent leaks – Multiple leaks suggest systemic failure
  7. Moss or algae growth – Traps moisture and damages roofing materials
  8. Higher energy bills – Poor ventilation and insulation drive up costs
  9. Roof age over 20 years – Most shingles reach end of life by this point
  10. Rotting or mold – Water damage inside your home is a serious warning

What To Do Next

If you notice any of these signs, schedule a professional inspection. A qualified roofer can assess whether repair or replacement is the best option.
